I am a Postdoctoral Research Associate at the Center for Quantum and Topological Systems (CQTS), New York University Abu Dhabi, and a Research Team Lead at the eBRAIN Lab.

My work focuses on Quantum Machine Learning, with a particular interest in noise-resilient Quantum Neural Networks, hybrid quantum‚Äìclassical architectures, and expressibility vs trainability trade-offs.
